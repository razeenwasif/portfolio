# Design System: Kinetic Intelligence Hub

## Overview
A high-performance, low-level engineering aesthetic designed for the deep tech sector. 
**Creative North Star:** The Kinetic Intelligence Hub (Obsidian Glass + Cyber Lime).

## Colors
- **Base:** Obsidian Glass (#0A0A0A)
- **Primary:** Cyber Lime (#BFFF00) for actions and metrics.
- **Secondary:** Vivid Violet (#9F00FF) for data categories.
- **Surface:** Deep gradients from #131314 to #0E0E0F.

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Space Grotesk (72px, 40px) - Technical-Editorial, tight tracking.
- **Body:** JetBrains Mono (18px, 16px) - Monospaced, engineering focus.

## Layout
- **Asymmetrical Bento Grid** with 24px gutters.
- **Glassmorphism:** 20px background blur on cards with 1px light-catching edges.

## Components
- **Buttons:** Solid Cyber Lime with black mono text.
- **Cards:** 15% opacity glass, 20px blur, 1px inner stroke.
- **Timeline:** A custom research-focused vertical feed.
